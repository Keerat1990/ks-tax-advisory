About us

Local, straightforward, and across the detail.

KS Tax Advisory is a Doreen-based practice offering tax and advisory services for individuals, companies, trusts and partnerships. We handle the compliance work properly — returns lodged correctly and on time — and go further with practical advice on structuring, planning and the decisions that shape your financial position year to year.

Whether it's your first personal return, a growing company's obligations, or a trust structure that needs an annual review, you'll deal directly with someone who takes the time to understand your situation before recommending anything.
