About us

Local roots, national reach.

KS Tax Advisory is a registered tax agent (TPB 25992341) and Xero Silver Partner, based in Doreen, Victoria and working with individuals, companies, trusts and partnerships right across Australia. We handle the compliance work properly — returns lodged correctly and on time — and go further with practical advice on structuring, planning and the decisions that shape your financial position year to year.

Whether it's your first personal return, a growing company's obligations, or a trust structure that needs an annual review, you'll deal directly with someone who takes the time to understand your situation before recommending anything — in person if you're nearby, or by phone and video if you're anywhere else in the country.

Good to know

Common questions.

Does KS Tax Advisory work with clients outside Doreen?

Yes — we're based in Doreen, VIC, but work with individuals, companies and trusts right across Australia by phone and video, with in-person meetings available for local clients.

Is KS Tax Advisory a registered tax agent?

Yes. We're registered with the Tax Practitioners Board (TPB registration 25992341) and are a Xero Silver Partner.

Can I complete everything remotely?

Yes. The 2026 tax form, document collection and booking are all handled online, so you can be onboarded from anywhere in the country.
